Noah’s Ark Dog & Cat Shelter Inc.

Location: Mabalacat City, Pampanga

Noah’s Ark Dog & Cat Shelter Inc. is a non-profit animal rescue organization based in Mabalacat City, Pampanga. Dedicated to saving stray and abandoned animals, the shelter provides a safe haven for dogs and cats in need. Through rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption programs, Noah’s Ark aims to find loving homes for every rescued pet. The shelter also advocates for responsible pet ownership, spay and neuter programs, and community education to promote animal welfare.

Best Friends Furever Sanctuary Inc.

Location: Magalang, Pampanga

Best Friends Furever Sanctuary Inc. is a non-profit animal rescue organization based in Magalang, Pampanga. Committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming stray and abandoned animals, the sanctuary provides a safe and loving environment for pets in need. Through adoption programs, medical care, and community education, Best Friends Furever Sanctuary promotes responsible pet ownership and advocates for animal welfare. Their mission is to give every rescued animal a second chance at a happy and fulfilling life.

Lyka’s Dog & cat Shelter

Location: Mabalacat City, Pampanga

LYKA’s Dog & Cat Shelter is a non-profit rescue in Angeles City, Philippines, dedicated to saving and caring for stray and abandoned animals. They provide medical care, food, and shelter while finding loving homes for rescued pets. Their mission is to promote animal welfare through rescue, rehabilitation, and responsible pet adoption. They also advocate for spaying and neutering to help control the stray animal population.
