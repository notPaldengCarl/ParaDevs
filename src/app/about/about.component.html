ABOUT US

Pampanga Animal Resources & Advocacy (PARA) is a dedicated group of individuals committed to advocating the rights and welfare of animals. Our mission is to promote responsible pet ownership, fight against animal cruelty, and encourage ethical treatment of all animals through education, and promoting local animal shelters in the province.

OUR VALUES

COMMUNITY

COMPASSION

ACCOUNTABILITY

OUR ADVOCACY

Our advocacy focuses on ending animal cruelty, fighting against animal testing, raising public awareness about responsible pet ownership, and addressing the rising stray animal population. We are dedicated to protecting and improving the lives of animals through education and promoting local animal shelters in Pampanga through the Adopt Don't Shop campaign. We encourage people to support spaying and neutering programs to control the stray population.
